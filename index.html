About Coffeemetry

Leading the way in coffee consulting and barista training excellence

Founded in 2015, Coffeemetry has grown from a small team of coffee enthusiasts to a premier consulting and training company serving coffee businesses across the Middle East and beyond. Our journey began with a simple belief: that every coffee shop has the potential to become a destination for exceptional coffee experiences.

Today, we are proud to have helped over 200 coffee businesses achieve their goals through our comprehensive consulting services, expert training programs, and innovative solutions. Our team of industry experts brings decades of combined experience in coffee production, business management, and education.
