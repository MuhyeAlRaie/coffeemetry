About Us

Our Mission & Vision

The core principles that guide everything we do at Coffeemetry

Our Mission

To empower coffee businesses with expert knowledge, practical skills, and innovative solutions for sustainable growth and exceptional customer experiences.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in coffee business consulting and barista education, setting industry standards for excellence and innovation.

Our Story

Coffeemetry is a leading coffee consulting and barista training company dedicated to helping coffee businesses thrive

Founded in 2015, Coffeemetry has grown from a small team of coffee enthusiasts to a premier consulting and training company serving coffee businesses across the Middle East and beyond. Our journey began with a simple belief: that every coffee shop has the potential to become a destination for exceptional coffee experiences.

Today, we are proud to have helped over 200 coffee businesses achieve their goals through our comprehensive consulting services, expert training programs, and innovative solutions. Our team of industry experts brings decades of combined experience in coffee production, business management, and education.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Coffeemetry

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every aspect of our work, from consulting services to training programs. Our commitment to quality ensures that our clients receive the best possible support for their coffee businesses.

Innovation

We embrace innovation and continuously seek new ways to improve our services and help our clients stay ahead in a competitive market. Our forward-thinking approach ensures that our clients benefit from the latest industry trends and technologies.

Integrity

We conduct our business with the highest level of integrity, transparency, and ethical standards. Our clients trust us to provide honest advice and recommendations that are in their best interest, not ours.

Sustainability

We are committed to promoting sustainable practices in the coffee industry. From ethical sourcing to environmentally friendly operations, we help our clients build businesses that are not only profitable but also responsible.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of collaboration and work closely with our clients to understand their unique challenges and goals. Our partnership approach ensures that solutions are tailored to each client's specific needs.

Education

We are passionate about coffee education and believe that knowledge is the key to success in the coffee industry. Our training programs are designed to empower coffee professionals with the skills and expertise they need to excel.
